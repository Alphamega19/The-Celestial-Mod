This is the music mod meant to be played with "The Celestial Mod"

Currently the mod adds four tracks to the game:

The Great Beyond - Celestial Mod Main Theme (Created by me) (The track only plays via a music box you can craft)
Magnetic Magnitude - Theme of the Magnitite Mistress (Created by HelplessHarp)
Gelatine Revenge - Theme of Expert King Slime (Created by me)
The Eye's Gaze - Theme of Expert Eye of Cthulhu(Created by me)
